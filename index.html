About Me

I’m a final-year Cybersecurity BSc (Hons) student at Bath Spa University with hands-on experience in cloud security, infrastructure, and cybersecurity.

I recently completed an internship at Microsoft, where I gained practical experience in cloud reliability, enterprise security assessments, and large-scale data handling. I earned CompTIA Tech+ and Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900) certifications. I delivered a capstone project on processing real-time F1 telemetry data in Azure, which I presented in a 30-minute technical session to peers and mentors. I also completed a mock cloud reliability assessment and peer-reviewed an enterprise security assessment, building practical insight into cloud resilience and security architecture at scale.

I’ve worked as a Teaching Assistant in Computing, mentoring peers in the lower years of Bath Spa University in C++ programming, digital forensics, and cybersecurity. I also founded Nodemine, a hosting service that I designed and operate end-to-end, giving me real-world experience in building, securing, and maintaining cloud-based infrastructure.
